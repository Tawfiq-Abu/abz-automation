Products

Find Out More Products

Our flagship product is AutoX, an automatic water pump controller and water management system. This is an automatic device designed to control the operation of an electric water pump for household, business and industrial use. When your water level gets below a predetermined level it switches on your pump automatically and when the tank is full it switches off the pump before the water overflows. It also has an added future which displays your water level and pump status at all times. This helps you to save water, electricity and money.
